	Social Media Do's and Don'ts To promote responsible digital citizenship, I created the following guidelines for college students:
  
Do’s: 
1.	Privacy – keep your profile private or semi – private 
2.	Professionalism – Thing before you post – ask: “would my lecturer see this ?’
3.	Fact checking– Verify information before sharing or reposting 
4.	Positive conduct – use social media to share achievements and learning
5.	Engagement – Engage respectfully in discussions and debates.

Don’ts: 
1.	Personal info – Never share your address phone number, or schedule
2.	Hate Speech – Do not post offensive , racist , or hateful content
3.	Cyberbullying- do not target,mock, or harass classmates online
4.	Oversharing – Do not post your real-time location or travel plans 
5.	Illegal Content – Do not share pirated content , explicit material , or defamatory.
